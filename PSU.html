Power Supply Unit

The Power Supply Unit, known as PSU acts as the main power supply for the whole computer. It plays a really important role because without the PSU a computer would never startup. Power supply comes in different voltages and one should buy the correct voltage power supply for their computers.
  PSU also comes in different grades and sizes. The grades including Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum. The better the grades are, the better the metal efficiency of the power supply unit but also at the same time, more expensive. It also comes in different sizes too so you should definitely check out if the PSU will fit in your computer casing before buying one.
